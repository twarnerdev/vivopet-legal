How do I add a new pet?
From the Home screen, tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Fill in your pet's details including name, species, breed, and birthday, then tap "Add Pet".
Can I share my pet's profile with family members?
Yes! Open your pet's profile, tap the share icon, and enter the email address of the person you want to share with. They'll receive access to view and manage your pet's information.
How do I set up medication reminders?
Go to your pet's profile, tap "Medications", then add a new medication. You can set the frequency and time for reminders, and we'll notify you when it's time for each dose.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage to protect your data. Your pet's information is private and only shared with people you explicitly authorize.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including pets, health records, and documents. This action cannot be undone.
Can I export my pet's health records?
You can view and share individual documents and records from within the app. We're working on a comprehensive export feature for future updates.
